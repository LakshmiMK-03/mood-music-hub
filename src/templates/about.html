About the Project

Emotion and Stress Aware Music Recommendation System — A Web-Based Application for Mental Well-Being

Purpose

Our purpose is to help people understand their emotions and manage stress through music.

This system analyzes a user's mood using text, voice, or facial expressions and provides personalized music recommendations along with simple relaxation tips.

We aim to make emotional support accessible, intelligent, and easy to use for everyone.

🌍 Vision

Our vision is to build a smart platform where technology understands human emotions and supports mental well-being.

We believe that music has the power to heal, calm, and motivate — and with the help of Artificial Intelligence, we can deliver the right music at the right time.

🎯 Mission

Our mission is to:

  • 1 Detect user emotions accurately using intelligent technology such as NLP, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision.
  • 2 Recommend music that matches the user's current mood for emotional regulation and stress relief.
  • 3 Provide helpful relaxation tips to reduce stress and improve focus through breathing exercises and mindfulness.
  • 4 Create a simple, user-friendly system that supports everyday mental wellness for everyone.
