How Emotion-to-Music Mapping Works

After the system detects the user's emotional state using NLP, ML, or Computer Vision techniques, it maps the classified emotion to a predefined music playlist. For example, if a user is detected as Sad, calm and peaceful tracks are recommended to soothe the mood. If Happy, energetic and upbeat music is suggested to match the positive vibe. This intelligent mapping uses psychological research on music therapy to promote emotional regulation.
